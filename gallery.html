A vocabulary for the sites you want.

Add key=value words to a /flow brief and each binds to real library knowledge — a structure playbook, an art-direction archetype, a motion and imagery stance. So structure=bento reliably means the bento playbook, not whatever the model improvises. Free text still works; the vocabulary is a menu, not a cage.

Your brieffree text — edit freely
structure=the page's spine
archetype=the whole art direction
imagery=how visuals are made
motion=how it moves
density=ideas per screen

Tip: click a selected chip again to clear it — an omitted axis isn't constrained.

your prompt
Paste this into Loupe's chat with experimental features on. Each key=value unfolds host-side into a binding block appended to your brief — unknown values are reported, never silently dropped.

Reference

The whole vocabulary.

Every term is bound to knowledge in the bundled library. Mix them freely — or leave any axis out.

structure= — the page's spine

bentoA span-choreographed bento; every tile earns its cell with a drawn micro-visualization.
posterA sequence of full-viewport typographic posters — huge scale moments, inversion as punctuation.
diagramThe core section IS a diagram: nodes in a grid with real SVG arrows drawn on scroll.
editorialA long-form spine under strict split discipline — never 50/50 twice, one full-bleed break.
dwellA pinned scroll-dwell sequence — a sticky stage over a tall track, lit by scroll progress.
marqueeA kinetic outline-type marquee band divides sections at poster scale, whisper weight.
numeral-railSteps ride an oversized outline-numeral rail dwelling beside their copy.
demo-heroThe hero performs the product: a chrome-framed miniature runs a scripted loop.

archetype= — the whole art direction

engineer-gridFlat paper + ink, hairlines instead of shadows, a machine-voice mono for labels, one prism accent.
luxury-darkNear-black ground, one metallic accent, serif display, letter-spaced caps, slow confident motion.
brutalist-techRaw visible grids, extreme type-scale contrast, black/white + one signal color, zero rounding.
swiss-gridStrict 12-column grid, one saturated accent, grotesk sans, whitespace as the material.
retro-terminalPhosphor mono on near-black, scanline texture, terminal chrome, data-dense HUD blocks.
editorial-serifMagazine serif display + humanist sans, column rules, drop caps, warm paper, photography-led.
soft-organicCream ground, rounded organic shapes, gentle tonal gradients in one hue, airy humane copy.

imagery=

photoReal licensed photography in the hero and key sections.
illustrationCSS/SVG illustrations only — deliberate drawn visuals and diagrams.
minimalTypography-led — layout, type and color carry the design.

motion= · density=

calmTransform-only reveals, precise hovers, one quiet continuous system.
richScroll-driven reveals, a kinetic system, hover physics — everything eased.
webglA WebGL hero/background moment plus smooth scrolling, with a static fallback.
airyFew ideas per viewport, oversized whitespace, one statement per section.
densePacked information design — tight grids, data readouts, micro-labels.
